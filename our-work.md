Our Works
We have accomplished lots of petty works at small scales across different parts of Nepal. Our members have been making impacts to plenty of farmer’s growth and livelihood at individual level and we shall continue to do that. As an organization too SADP Nepal has successfully completed enough social works focusing on sustainable organic farming in different parts of Nepal. Few of our noteworthy works are listed here.

Climate change and coffee production project (Panchkhal, Kavre district)

Name of the project: Climate resilient farming to combat climate change in Hokse, Panchkhal, Kavre district of Nepal (2025-2026) supported by UNCON, USA

SADP Nepal completed climate change and coffee production orientation program to farmers of Panchkhal in presence of Deputy Mayor of Panchkhal Municipality on November 2, 2025. The program was successfully conducted and effectively met its objectives. Active participation, practical knowledge sharing, and strong cooperation among stakeholders contributed to the program’s success. Follow-up activities such as field demonstrations and additional training sessions are recommended to support the practical adoption of the practices shared during the program. Very soon, SADP is going to conduct 2 days coffee production training to 25 farmers.

Marketing of Organic Products Program (KOSELI GHAR Project)- 2023-2024

The project was implemented from 2022 to 2023 in Sanga, Kavre for the installation of organic shop in concept of promotion of Eco-tourism. The training on grading, packaging was provided to the women for the employment generation and supported with seed money to start the organic shop. The seed money was returned back to another woman for initiating such business.

Livelihood Generation Project

Title of the project: Recognition of Importance of Women through economic empowerment in Agriculture in Kavre district of Nepal

From November, 2020, an income generation project was initiated from Sanga, Kavre district of Nepal. The project was funded by UNCON, USA. 10 women were benefitted by the project by farming goat and poultry. The project is on-going and will end in October, 2021.



Extending shelf-life and quality of vegetables through the construction of cost-effective storage unit for marketing purpose, Trellis Fund, USAID, 2017-2018

The project funded by USAID, Trellis Fund for the construction of Zero Energy Cool Chamber (ZECC) in Kavre district for the marketing of the green vegetables benefitted more than 100 families to store and extend the shelf-life.



Earthquake Victim Relief Programs

Earthquake that hit on 25th April, 2015 have destroyed most of the part of Nepal. On regard to that team of SADP is concentrated on supporting victims of Tanahun district and Kavre district. We have collaborated with Bumblebee Foundation of Australia/United Religions Initiatives (URI) and different individual donors around the world.  With little support it worth a lot for the people of Tanahu and Kavre district. Lets join hand together to support  needy people in rehabilitation.
